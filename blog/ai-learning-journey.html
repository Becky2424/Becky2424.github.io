My Journey into AI: Why I Decided to Learn

February 10, 2026

After more than 20 years in operations management, I made a decision that surprised a lot of people: I enrolled in an AI native project development program. Here is why I did it, and what I have learned so far.

Why AI? Why Now?

I have spent my career solving operational problems, managing teams, and finding ways to do things more efficiently. When I started seeing how AI tools were transforming the way businesses operate, I knew I did not want to be left behind. I wanted to understand this technology, not just use it.

The Biggest Surprise

The biggest surprise has been how much my operations background actually helps. Understanding workflows, processes, and how teams function gives me a practical lens for thinking about where AI can make a real impact. I am not starting from scratch. I am building on decades of experience.

What I Am Learning

Right now, I am focused on understanding how to work with AI as a tool for project development. This includes:

  • Learning how AI models work at a high level
  • Understanding prompt engineering and how to communicate effectively with AI
  • Exploring how AI can streamline operations and decision-making
  • Building practical projects that combine my industry knowledge with new technology

Advice for Others Considering the Leap

If you are a mid-career professional thinking about learning AI, my advice is simple: start. You do not need a computer science degree. You do not need to be in your twenties. What you need is curiosity, a willingness to be a beginner again, and the understanding that your existing experience is an asset, not a limitation.

I will be sharing more about my learning journey here on this blog. Stay tuned.
